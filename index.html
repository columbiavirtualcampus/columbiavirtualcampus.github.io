Columbia University
VIRTUAL CAMPUS
Design Challenge
JOIN US!
Join us April 4, 5 and 6 to build virtual solutions to the challenges of remote living.

Columbia students from all majors are called upon to design solutions to all aspects of campus life ranging from academics to mental health.

No prior experience is necessary to participate. We will introduce you to design, help you form teams, and provide expert mentors.

Prizes will be awarded for winning designs.

GET INVOLVED
Students, please register here.
Sponsors, please reach out to Prof. Lydia Chilton (chilton@cs.columbia.edu) to get involved.
