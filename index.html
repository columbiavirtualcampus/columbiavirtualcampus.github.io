JOIN US!
Want to help with the COVID-19 crisis from home?
Join us to address the challenges of remote living.
Together, we will design and build virtual solutions to all aspects of campus life.

When: April 4, 5 and 6
Where: Zoom
Who: All Columbia and Barnard students
Register: On the Facebook Page

No experience necessary.
We will introduce you to design, help you form teams, and provide expert mentors.

$500 prizes will be awarded for the winning designs.
